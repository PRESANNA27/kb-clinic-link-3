Partner Units

Our Associated Services

Three expert service units co-located with KB Clinic — giving you comprehensive, one-stop healthcare.

Bama Medicals
On-site Pharmacy
Bama Medicals is a full-service pharmacy co-located with KB Clinic, ensuring you receive your prescriptions instantly after every consultation — no extra trips. We stock a comprehensive range of medicines including branded generics, specialist medications, and over-the-counter products.
  • All prescription medicines available on-site immediately after consultation
  • Branded & quality generic medicines at affordable prices
  • Diabetes supplies: insulin, strips, lancets, and glucose monitors
  • Specialist ENT medications, ear drops, nasal sprays
  • Knowledgeable staff for medication counselling and adherence support
  • Cold chain maintained for temperature-sensitive medications
Bala Diagnostics
Clinical Laboratory
Bala Diagnostics is KB Clinic's dedicated in-house laboratory offering a comprehensive suite of pathology, biochemistry, haematology, and microbiology tests — with same-day results for most investigations. Our lab meets the highest quality standards to support accurate diagnosis.
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC), ESR, and haematology panel
  • Diabetes: HbA1c, fasting & post-prandial glucose, insulin levels
  • Renal & liver function tests — same-day results
  • Thyroid profile: TSH, T3, T4, anti-TPO
  • Lipid profile, cardiac markers, uric acid
  • Home sample collection available — call 89259 29922
A.M. Audiology & Hearing Aid Centre
Hearing Rehabilitation
A.M. Audiology & Hearing Aid Centre provides expert audiological evaluation, hearing aid fitting, and complete after-sales service. Working closely with Dr. Ganesh Bala, our audiologist delivers precise hearing rehabilitation for patients of all ages — from children to the elderly.
  • Pure-tone audiometry & speech audiometry testing
  • Digital & rechargeable hearing aid fitting from leading brands
  • Custom ear mould fabrication for optimal fit and comfort
  • Hearing aid programming, fine-tuning, and regular servicing
  • Paediatric hearing assessment and rehabilitation
  • Tinnitus management counselling and devices
